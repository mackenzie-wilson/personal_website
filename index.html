About Me


My current role specializes in business intelligence developing tools that provide process improvement consultants the ability to analyze and visualize program-based processes in real time. Additionally, my team offers businesss intelligence services to other divisions on an ad hoc basis. I also have experience in project management, risk and fraud predictive analytics, and complex asset management.

The most rewarding projects I have been a part of involved building tools (internal products) that help make my colleague’s work lives better. While my current position is rewarding and challenging, my passion and aspirations lie in building products.

Originally, I was aiming to break into product management to build the skills needed to realize my entrepreneurial goals. Taking a more roundabout way, I’ve decided to just go ahead and start building products alongside a developer as a method to hone and showcase my product management expertise for potential employers.

I will be writing posts documenting the product management lifecycle of each project, along with all the books, tools, and frameworks used in each of the three stages of the lean startup process (business model design, customer development, and agile engineering). If you would like to know where we are at in development, navigate to the Projects or Blog pages.
