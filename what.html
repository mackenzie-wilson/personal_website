What is CheapNite?

March 13th, 2023

Product Management | Design | Development

CheapNite is a digital marketing platform aimed at helping users find and utilize restaurant deals, specials, and promotions in their local area. Cheapnite is also a great platform for restaurants to promote their specials and deals to customers living in, or traveling through their city at an affordable price.

The idea for CheapNite was born from a problem experienced by two hungry university students looking for somewhere to sit down for cheap food. Finding a simple google search unfruitful, they wondered why there wasn't a central spot to find local sit-down deals in their immediate area. They pitched the idea to the university's startup incubator program and was accepted to the cohort. Specializing in business, the two ended up seeking people for the technology side of things.

One of the student's older brothers had just graduated with a degree in Computer Science and was looking for a development project to bolster his resume and add to his portfolio. Knowing I was in the same boat, he suggested we join the founding team of CheapNite to help develop their product.

Areas I was looking to improve were my weak points whenever I was applying and interviewing for poduct management roles. I had a strong analytical background, done some basic design and development, but never work alongside a developer and designer while influencing without authority. We figured the best way for us to both benefit was to have him lead software development while I lead product, both contributing to design, product management, and development in varying capacities to maximize business value and communication with the business, marketing, and sales team.
